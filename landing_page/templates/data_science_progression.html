Thinking about a Career in Data Science?

My recommended subjects if you want to be in Data Science. I would suggest doing a 4/5 year degree, as there are so many subjects that are useful in addition to this list.

I assume that you will be majoring in statistics (the new Data Science does not seem appealing to me).

If you are heavily into the theoretical side and want to be involved in research, majoring in math and computer science may provide a stronger foundation.

This is the list of subjects I would have compeleted in hindsight given a 3 year degree. As I mentioned before, I would suggest at least 4 year degree.
